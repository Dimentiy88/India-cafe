About

  • India Cafe serves the Chambersburg area with delicious indian cuisine. Our specialty dishes have been well-crafted to create a delightful culinary experience. Enjoy the convenience of pickup or delivery when ordering through Beyond Menu.

  • Community and Culture: Beyond the confines of our kitchen, India Cafe is closely connected with the local community. We believe in fostering relationships and sharing the joy of good food. From cultural events to community outreach, we aim to be more than just a cafe; we strive to create an atmosphere of belonging and celebration.

  • Our talented chefs at India Cafe are masters of Indian cuisine, each bringing their own unique touch to create our signature dishes. They carefully select spices and ingredients to ensure that each dish is not only delicious, but also reflects the rich cultural mosaic of India. With their unmatched skill and passion for cooking, our chefs ensure that every visit to India Cafe is an unforgettable culinary journey

  • Culinary Adventure: India Cafe transforms every visit into not just a meal, but a tour of India’s flavors. Our menu reflects the richness of various regional traditions – from the spicy seasonings of the North to the coastal gastronomic pleasures of the South. Each dish is a story that invites you to experience the rich culture and authenticity encapsulated in every bite.
